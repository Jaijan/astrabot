# 🚀 About

**Astrabot** is a next-generation AI-powered career discovery platform inspired by expert-system style questioning.

Think of any profession.

Astrabot will intelligently narrow down possibilities through adaptive questions, identify the profession you're thinking of, and then become your personal AI career mentor by providing:

- 🎯 Career Guidance
- 🛣️ Learning Roadmaps
- 💼 Salary Insights
- 📈 Market Demand
- 📚 Learning Resources
- 🎓 Certifications
- 🧠 Skill Development
- 🚀 Career Growth
- 🤖 AI Impact
- 🌌 Related Career Exploration

Unlike traditional guessing games, Astrabot continues the experience after identifying the profession by helping users understand and navigate their chosen career path.

## 🧬 Career DNA

Astrabot builds a personality profile while asking questions.

Example attributes include

- Analytical Thinking
- Leadership
- Creativity
- Communication
- Problem Solving
- Technical Aptitude
- Entrepreneurship
- Risk Taking

These insights are used to recommend similar professions.

---

## 🧠 Self Learning AI

When Astrabot cannot correctly identify a profession

It asks

> What profession were you thinking of?

followed by

> What question would have distinguished it?

The system stores the new knowledge and continuously improves over time.
